# Oncology Clinical Trial Analysis

## Objective
To analyze global oncology clinical trials using publicly available data from ClinicalTrials.gov.

---

## Dataset
- Source: ClinicalTrials.gov API
- Size: ~5000 trials
- Focus: Oncology-related studies

---

## Key Analyses
- Distribution of trial statuses
- Distribution of trial phases
- Geographic distribution of trials
- Enrollment size distribution
- Trends in trials over time
- Top sponsoring organizations

---

## Key Insights
- Majority of trials are completed or recruiting
- Phase 2 trials are the most common
- The United States dominates trial activity
- Enrollment sizes vary widely, with many small trials and fewer large-scale studies
- Significant growth in trials after 2010
